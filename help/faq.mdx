<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is PLR content, and why does it need personalizing?">
    PLR (Private Label Rights) content is pre-written articles or posts you buy the rights to publish as your own. The catch is that thousands of other owners buy the same packs, so without personalization your content sounds identical to everyone else's. BrandVoice Pro rewrites it with your story, voice, and brand woven throughout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the SPEAK Method?">
    The SPEAK Method is BrandVoice Pro's proprietary five-dimension personalization framework, developed by writer and editor Michele Barard. It captures the full identity behind your brand, not just a style preference. The complete framework is available to members inside the app. See [The SPEAK Method](/speak-method).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to use PLR content?">
    No. BrandVoice Pro is optimized for PLR, but it works equally well for rewriting your own drafts, refreshing older posts, or generating branded content from scratch.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as one generation?">
    One article personalization is one generation. One social post request is one generation, no matter how many platforms it covers. Articles and social posts share the same monthly pool. See [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does my monthly generation pool reset?">
    Free plans reset on the 1st of each calendar month (UTC). Basic and Premium reset on your monthly billing anniversary — the day of the month you first subscribed, applied every month (including for annual subscribers). If that day doesn't exist in a shorter month, the reset falls on the last day of that month. The usage banner in the app shows your personal reset date, for example **"Your allowance resets Aug 14"**. See [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which social platforms are supported?">
    Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Threads, and X. Each post is tailored to that platform's tone and format, generated together in a single step.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I manage more than one brand?">
    Yes. Save multiple Brand Profiles and switch between them in one click. The number of profiles depends on your plan: 1 on Free, up to 3 on Basic, and up to 10 on Premium.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. The free plan includes 6 generations per month and 1 Brand Profile, with no credit card required. It includes social posts and the optional TL;DR and FAQ extras.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does beta pricing mean?">
    BrandVoice Pro is in beta ahead of public launch. Sign up during beta to lock in your rate permanently. It never increases as long as you stay on your plan. Changing tiers forfeits the locked-in rate. See [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
