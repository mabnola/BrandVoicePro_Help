<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is PLR content, and why does it need personalizing?">
    PLR (Private Label Rights) content is pre-written articles or posts you buy the rights to publish as your own. The catch is that thousands of other owners buy the same packs, so without personalization your content sounds identical to everyone else's. BrandVoice Pro rewrites it with your story, voice, and brand woven throughout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the SPEAK Method?">
    The SPEAK Method is BrandVoice Pro's proprietary five-dimension personalization framework, developed by writer and editor Michele Barard. It captures the full identity behind your brand, not just a style preference. The complete framework is available to members inside the app. See [The SPEAK Method](/speak-method).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to use PLR content?">
    No. BrandVoice Pro is optimized for PLR, but it works equally well for rewriting your own drafts, refreshing older posts, or building branded content from a rough outline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as one generation?">
    One article personalization is one generation. One social post request is one generation, no matter how many platforms it covers. Articles and social posts share the same monthly pool, which resets at the start of each calendar month. SEO title suggestions are free. See [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which social platforms are supported?">
    Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, TikTok, and Threads. Each post is written for that platform's length and culture, and every platform you select comes back in a single request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long can my source content be?">
    The limit is per generation and depends on your plan: 1,500 words on Free, 3,000 on Basic, 5,000 on Premium. It applies to the text you paste in. The app counts your words as you type and warns you before you generate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I manage more than one brand?">
    Yes. Save multiple Brand Profiles and switch between them in one click. The number depends on your plan: 1 on Free, up to 3 on Basic, up to 10 on Premium.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. The free plan includes 6 generations per month, 1,500 words per generation, and 1 Brand Profile, with no credit card required. It includes social posts for all six platforms and free SEO title options. The full Brand Profile and the TL;DR and FAQ extras are on paid plans.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get TL;DR and FAQ sections?">
    They are part of the SPEAK Boost extras on the Premium plan. Set them as a default on a Brand Profile, or turn them on for a single piece from the SPEAK Boost panel. See [SEO extras](/using/seo-extras).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial on paid plans?">
    Yes. Every paid plan starts with a 7-day free trial, and you are not charged until it ends. Cancel anytime from the billing portal. See [Billing](/plans/billing).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does beta pricing mean?">
    BrandVoice Pro is in beta ahead of public launch. Members who subscribed during beta hold a locked-in rate below current pricing. Any completed tier change, upgrade or downgrade, permanently forfeits that rate. See [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
