Most issues fall into a few categories. Try the fixes below, and if you are still stuck, contact support.

## My content is longer than the word limit

Each plan has a per-generation word limit: 1,500 words on Free, 3,000 on Basic, and 5,000 on Premium. If your piece is over the limit, either split it into sections and personalize them separately, or upgrade to a plan with a higher limit. See [Plans & Pricing](/plans/plans-and-pricing).

## I ran out of generations

When your pool refreshes depends on your plan. Free plans reset on the 1st of each calendar month (UTC). Basic and Premium reset on your monthly billing anniversary — the same day of the month you first subscribed, including for annual subscribers. If your anniversary day doesn't exist in a shorter month, the reset lands on the last day of that month.

The usage banner in the app shows your personal reset date (for example, **"Your allowance resets Aug 14"**), so you know exactly when more generations become available. If you need more before then, you can upgrade at any time. Before upgrading, review how tier changes affect a locked-in beta rate on [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering).

## The output does not sound like me yet

The single biggest factor is your Brand Profile. Vague profiles produce generic output. Sharpen your origin story and highlight message, then generate again. See [Brand Profiles](/using/brand-profiles) for what to include.

## I can't switch Brand Profiles

Check your plan's profile limit: 1 on Free, up to 3 on Basic, and up to 10 on Premium. If you are at your limit, you can edit an existing profile or upgrade for more slots.

## My social posts look similar across platforms

Each platform's post is tailored to its own tone and format. If they feel too alike, a more specific Brand Profile and highlight message give the app more to differentiate with.

## Contact support

If none of these solve it, email support with your account email and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened. Screenshots help.
