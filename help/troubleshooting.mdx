Most issues fall into a few categories. Try the fixes below, and if you are still stuck, contact support.

## My content is longer than the word limit

Each plan has a per-generation word limit on the text you paste in: 1,500 words on Free, 3,000 on Basic, 5,000 on Premium. The counter under the source box turns red when you go over, and the Generate button stays disabled. Either split the piece into sections and personalize them separately, or upgrade for a higher limit. A blocked request does not use a generation. See [Plans & Pricing](/plans/plans-and-pricing).

## I ran out of generations

Your pool resets at the start of each calendar month, not on your billing date. If you need more before then, you can upgrade at any time. Before upgrading, review how tier changes affect a locked-in rate on [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering).

## The Generate button will not turn on

Check three things: a Brand Profile is selected in the sidebar, the source box has content in it, and your word count is under your plan limit. On the Collateral tab, at least one platform must be selected as well.

## My plan still shows the old tier after upgrading

Plan status is cached briefly after a change. Refresh the page, and if it is still wrong a few minutes later, sign out and back in. If it persists, email support with your account email.

## I cannot fill in parts of my Brand Profile

Brand voice, personality, niche, what you do, content goal, target audience, website, and CTA are advanced brand voice tuning fields, available on Basic and Premium. SPEAK Boost extras and the writing DOs and DON'Ts are Premium. Locked sections show an upgrade prompt in place of the fields. See [Brand Profiles](/using/brand-profiles).

## I cannot create another Brand Profile

Check your plan's profile limit: 1 on Free, up to 3 on Basic, up to 10 on Premium. At the limit, the new-profile button is replaced with an upgrade prompt. You can still edit or delete an existing profile.

## SEO titles did not appear

Titles are generated after an article personalization finishes, and they need an active Brand Profile. If the app reports that it could not generate the titles, run the personalization again. Titles are free, so a retry costs you nothing.

## My content is missing from History

A piece is added to History when you save or download it. If you only copied the output and left the tab, it was not stored. Save first, then copy.

## The output does not sound like me yet

The single biggest factor is your Brand Profile. Vague profiles produce generic output. Sharpen your origin story, highlight message, and audience description, then try the **deep** rewrite level. On Premium, the writing DOs and DON'Ts give you direct control over habits you want enforced or banned.

## My social posts look similar across platforms

Each platform's post is written to that platform's length and style. If they still feel too alike, a more specific Brand Profile and highlight message give the app more to differentiate with.

## Contact support

If none of these solve it, email support with your account email and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened. Screenshots help.
