## What you can do here

- Personalize PLR articles so they carry your real story and voice
- Generate platform-specific social posts for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Threads, and X in a single step
- Save multiple Brand Profiles and switch between them in one click
- Add optional TL;DR and FAQ sections built for AI search visibility
