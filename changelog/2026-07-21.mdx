## Updates

**Paid plans reset on your billing date**

If you're on Basic or Premium, your generation allowance now refreshes on the same day each month as your billing date — not on the first of the calendar month. Annual subscribers get a fresh allowance every month, on the same day their subscription started.

Free plans continue to reset on the first of each calendar month.

The limit-reached banners on the Customize and Collateral tabs now tell you the exact date your allowance resets (for example, "Your allowance resets Aug 14"), so there's no guesswork about when you can generate again.

See [Generations explained](/plans/generations-explained) for how the pool works, and [Billing](/plans/billing) for how billing dates are set.
