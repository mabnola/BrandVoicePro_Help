## New features

**Send to Step 2 button.** After you rewrite a piece in Customize (Step 1), a new **Send to Step 2** button on the result header carries that content straight into Generate Collateral. No more copy/paste between steps. See [Social posts](/using/social-posts).

## Updates

**Cleaner social posts.** SPEAK Content Extras (TL;DR, FAQ, reflection questions, and similar add-ons) now stay on the long-form article in Step 1 and are no longer applied to social posts. Structure and Custom K still shape your posts. The SPEAK Boost panel in Collateral has been trimmed accordingly. Learn more in [SEO extras](/using/seo-extras).

## Bug fixes

**X (Twitter) CTAs respect the character limit.** The model now includes your call-to-action on X only when the post still fits within 280 characters, and omits it otherwise so posts never get cut off.
