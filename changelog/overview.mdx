Product updates to BrandVoice Pro, newest first.

## Week of July 21, 2026

### New features

<Update label="July 21, 2026" tags={["new"]}>
  **Brand profile labels in History**

  If you manage more than one [Brand Profile](/using/brand-profiles), each item in your History now shows which profile it was created for. A small building icon and the profile name appear under the title, so you can tell at a glance whether an article or social post came from your coaching brand, your storefront, or any other profile.

  Single-profile users see no change — the label only appears when you have two or more profiles, so nothing new clutters the sidebar for solo brands.
</Update>
