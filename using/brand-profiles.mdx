A Brand Profile is the heart of BrandVoice Pro. It stores everything the app needs to make content sound like you, so you never have to re-explain your brand from scratch. Profiles are saved to your account, so they are available from any device you sign in on.

Create one from the **Brand Profiles** tab in the sidebar with **New Brand Profile**, then select it to make it active. The active profile is used by both the Customize and Collateral tabs.

## What a profile captures

Every account fills in these fields:

- **Profile name.** A label for you, such as "Main Brand" (required).
- **Company or brand name.** What your business is called (required).
- **Origin story.** Why you started, and what makes your perspective yours.
- **Specific story or message to highlight.** A result, anecdote, or point you want woven through the content.

Paid plans unlock the rest of the profile, shown in the app as advanced brand voice tuning:

- **Brand voice and tone.** How you sound in practice.
- **Brand personality.** Who your brand would be as a person.
- **Niche or industry.** The space you operate in.
- **What the company or brand does.** Your core product, service, or transformation.
- **Content goal.** Traffic, leads, conversions, authority, or retention.
- **Target audience.** Who you are writing for, as specifically as you can put it.
- **Website URL** and **default CTA.** Used where they fit naturally, especially in social posts.

The Premium plan adds two more sections:

- **Content extras, default structure, and a custom instruction.** Profile-level defaults for every generation, including TL;DR and FAQ. See [SEO extras](/using/seo-extras).
- **Writing DOs and DON'Ts.** Hard rules the writer follows on every piece, such as "never use em dashes."

<Tip>
  Spend the most time on your origin story, your highlight message, and your audience. They do the heavy lifting in making output feel authentic rather than generic.
</Tip>

## Saving and switching profiles

Select any profile in the sidebar to make it active, and your next generation uses it. There is no need to re-enter your story, audience, or goals each time. This is especially useful if you manage more than one brand or product line.

The number of profiles you can save depends on your plan:

| Plan | Saved Brand Profiles |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 1 |
| Basic | Up to 3 |
| Premium | Up to 10 |

When you hit the limit, the app replaces the **New Brand Profile** button with an upgrade prompt. You can still edit or delete the profiles you have. See [Plans & Pricing](/plans/plans-and-pricing) for the full comparison.

## Editing a profile

Hover a profile in the sidebar and select the settings icon to edit any field, or the trash icon to delete it. New generations use the latest saved version, so if your audience shifts or you refine your story, your content keeps pace.
