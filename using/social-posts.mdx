BrandVoice Pro writes social posts natively for each platform, not the same caption pasted six times. You will find them on the **Collateral** tab.

## Supported platforms

| Platform | What you get |
| --- | --- |
| Facebook | 200 to 500 words, conversational, ends on a question or CTA |
| Instagram | 150 to 300 words of visual storytelling, plus 10 to 15 hashtags |
| X (Twitter) | Under 280 characters, punchy, one or two hashtags |
| LinkedIn | 300 to 600 words, professional, thought-leadership framing |
| TikTok | A short caption plus a 30 to 60 second video script outline |
| Threads | Under 500 characters, conversational, works as a thread hook |

Facebook, Instagram, X, and LinkedIn are selected by default. Select or deselect any combination before you generate.

## How to generate social posts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a profile">
    The active [Brand Profile](/using/brand-profiles) supplies your voice, audience, and default CTA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your content">
    Paste the article, the personalized version from the Customize tab, or any source material you want the posts built from. The same per-plan word limit applies here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your platforms">
    Select the platforms you want. At least one is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    All selected platforms come back in one pass, split into a labeled block per platform, ready to copy or download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Collateral tab works on its own, so you do not have to personalize an article first when you just need to fill your social calendar.

## One request, one generation

A social post request counts as one generation no matter how many platforms it covers. Generating for all six still uses one generation from your monthly pool. See [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained).

<Tip>
  Because a full six-platform set costs the same as a single-platform set, select every platform you actually publish on before you hit Generate.
</Tip>
