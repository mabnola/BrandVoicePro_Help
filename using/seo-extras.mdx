BrandVoice Pro can add two optional sections to your content: a TL;DR summary and an FAQ. Both are designed to help your content get picked up by search engines and AI search tools like Perplexity and ChatGPT.

## What the extras do

- **TL;DR.** A short summary of the piece, useful for readers who skim and for search engines that surface concise answers.
- **FAQ.** A set of questions and answers structured around conversational, search-intent phrasing, which is the kind of content that earns featured snippets and shows up in AI search answers.

## Where the extras apply

TL;DR, FAQ, and the rest of the Content Extras (reflection questions, motivational quotes, personal-story hooks, relevant statistics, discussion questions) are applied to the **long-form article you generate in Step 1 (Customize)**. They are not added to social posts in Step 2, because a TL;DR or FAQ block doesn't belong inside a 280-character caption.

If you want that same shape on Step 2, use **Structure preference** — for example, choose *FAQ format* — or add a **Custom K** instruction. See [Social Posts](/using/social-posts) for how Step 2 works.

## Turning them on

You can enable the extras two ways:

- **Per profile.** Set a default so every article for that brand includes them.
- **Per article.** Toggle them on or off for an individual Step 1 generation.
