BrandVoice Pro includes two kinds of search-focused help: title suggestions that come with every article personalization, and optional TL;DR and FAQ sections available on the Premium plan.

## SEO title options

Every time an article personalization finishes, BrandVoice Pro generates five title options for the finished piece, informed by your brand name, niche, and target audience. The set mixes question, how-to, list, and statement formats, and aims for under 65 characters. Copy any title with one click.

<Note>
  Title suggestions are available on every plan and do not draw from your monthly generation pool.
</Note>

## TL;DR and FAQ sections

<Info>
  TL;DR and FAQ are part of the SPEAK Boost extras, which are available on the **Premium** plan.
</Info>

- **TL;DR.** A short summary of the piece, useful for readers who skim and for search engines that surface concise answers. In an article it lands at the end under a TL;DR heading; in a social post it becomes a single labeled line.
- **FAQ.** Three to five concise questions and answers at the end of an article, phrased the way people actually search, which is the kind of content that earns featured snippets and shows up in AI search answers. In a social post it becomes one or two brief Q&As.

Both work on articles and on social posts.

## Turning them on

Premium accounts can set them in two places, and both are available at the same time:

- **Per profile.** Set your defaults in the Brand Profile, and every generation for that brand includes them.
- **Per generation.** Open the **SPEAK Boost** panel on the Customize or Collateral tab to change the extras for one piece only. The panel loads your profile defaults, so you can add to them, or clear them to skip the extras for that piece.

Alongside TL;DR and FAQ, the same panel offers extras such as reflection questions, a motivational quote, a personal story hook, a relevant statistic, or a discussion question, plus a structure preference and a custom instruction of your own.
