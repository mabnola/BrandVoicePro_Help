Personalization is the core of BrandVoice Pro. You bring a generic piece, your Brand Profile does the rest, and the output reads like you wrote it from personal experience. It all happens on the **Customize** tab.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a profile">
    Select the [Brand Profile](/using/brand-profiles) you want to write as in the sidebar. A profile must be active before the Generate button turns on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your content">
    Drop your PLR article or draft into the source box. The counter under the box tracks your word count against your plan limit and turns red if you go over. The limit applies to the text you paste in, not the text that comes out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a rewrite level">
    - **Light.** Keeps the structure, shifts tone and vocabulary, replaces generic phrases.
    - **Moderate.** Restructures sentences and paragraphs, swaps generic examples for niche-relevant ones, injects personality throughout.
    - **Deep.** Rewrites from scratch: same core information, entirely original voice and framing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add focus areas (optional)">
    Nudge the rewrite with any combination of **Stronger Hook**, **Add Examples**, **Add CTAs**, **More Conversational**, and **Professional Tone**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    The rewrite streams in as it is written. Length is matched to your source, so a full-length article comes back as a full-length article rather than a summary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review, copy, or save">
    Copy the result with formatting intact for pasting into Word, Google Docs, or your CMS, or download it as **.txt**, **.md**, **.rtf**, or **.docx**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Saving a piece is what adds it to your History. Open the **History** tab in the sidebar to reload a past piece, along with the source you started from.
</Note>

## What makes it different from a rewrite

A generic AI rewrite changes words. BrandVoice Pro changes whose voice the piece is in. It does not just swap your name into a template thousands of others are publishing. It weaves your actual story and audience language throughout, so a reader feels like they know the person behind the brand.

Specific examples in your source are treated carefully. Statistics, named research, and case study results are preserved and reframed in your voice rather than invented over.

## SEO titles come free

When a rewrite finishes, BrandVoice Pro generates five SEO title options for the finished piece using your brand and audience. Titles do not use a generation from your monthly pool. See [SEO extras](/using/seo-extras).

## Works beyond PLR

BrandVoice Pro was built for the PLR personalization workflow, but it works just as well for rewriting your own drafts, refreshing older posts, or generating branded content from a rough outline. Any workflow where consistency and authenticity matter benefits from it.
