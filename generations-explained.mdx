Your plan gives you a set number of generations each month. Understanding what counts as one helps you get the most from your plan.

## What counts as one generation

- **One article personalization = one generation.** Personalizing a single PLR article or draft uses one generation.
- **One social post request = one generation.** A social post request uses one generation no matter how many platforms it covers. Generating posts for all six platforms at once still counts as one.

<Tip>
  Because a full six-platform social set is a single generation, generating your article and its matching social posts together is an efficient way to use your pool.
</Tip>

## The pool is shared

Articles and social posts draw from the same monthly pool. There is no separate allowance for each. You decide how to split your generations between long-form personalization and social content.

## Monthly limits by plan

| Plan | Generations / mo |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 6 (grandfathered free accounts: 10) |
| Basic | 50 |
| Premium | 150 |

## When the pool resets

Your generation pool refreshes at the start of each billing month. If you need more than your plan allows, you can upgrade at any time. Before you do, read [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering), because changing tiers affects any locked-in beta rate.
