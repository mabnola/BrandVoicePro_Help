Every personalization BrandVoice Pro produces runs on the SPEAK Method, a proprietary content personalization framework. Writer and editor Michele Barard developed it over two years of working with PLR content and AI writing tools.

Most tools capture style preferences: tone, formality, vocabulary. The SPEAK Method goes deeper. It captures the full identity behind your brand across five dimensions, giving the AI everything it needs to write in your voice, for your audience, every time.

<Note>
  SPEAK is built into the app, so you never have to learn it or apply it by hand. You fill in your Brand Profile, and the framework works in the background on every generation.
</Note>

## Why it matters

A tone preset answers one question: how should this sound? SPEAK answers a much deeper one: who is this person, what is their story, and how do they speak to their specific audience? Those produce very different results. "Casual" applied to any piece gives you the same generic register. A SPEAK profile gives you content that sounds like a specific real person, which is you.

That is the difference between content that merely exists and content readers trust, share, and come back for.

## The full framework

The complete SPEAK framework, with the full breakdown of all five dimensions and how to use each one, is available to members inside the app. It is the core intellectual property behind BrandVoice Pro, and every generation applies it for you automatically.
