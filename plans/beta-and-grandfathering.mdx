BrandVoice Pro is currently in beta ahead of its public launch. Members who subscribed during beta hold a locked-in rate below the current published pricing.

## What beta pricing means

If you subscribed during the beta period, your price is locked in and does not increase as new features are added. Beta rates are:

| Plan | Beta rate | Current standard rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | $19/mo, or $180/yr | $29/mo, or $288/yr |
| Premium | $39/mo, or $384/yr | $69/mo, or $708/yr |

Beta accounts get the same generation and word allowances as the standard plans: 50 generations and 3,000 words on Basic, 150 generations and 5,000 words on Premium. The advantage is the price.

<Note>
  When you are on a beta rate, the app shows it on your current plan card, labeled as your legacy rate.
</Note>

## The one rule that affects your locked-in rate

Your grandfathered rate holds as long as you stay on your current plan. **Any completed tier change, upgrade or downgrade, permanently forfeits grandfathered benefits.** After a tier change, your account is priced and metered at the current published rate for the new tier, and the earlier rate cannot be restored.

<Warning>
  This applies in both directions, and it applies to cancellations too. If you cancel a paid plan, your account moves to the current standard free plan. Consider carefully before switching plans, because a completed change cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Free accounts

Free accounts created before the free-plan generation change keep their earlier allowance of 10 generations per month. New free signups get 6 per month.

The same rule applies here: if a grandfathered free account subscribes to a paid plan, that legacy allowance is forfeited. Cancelling later returns the account to the current standard free plan at 6 generations per month, not to the earlier 10. See [Plans & Pricing](/plans/plans-and-pricing).
