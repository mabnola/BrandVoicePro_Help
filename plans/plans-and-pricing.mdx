BrandVoice Pro has three plans. Every plan includes article personalization, platform-specific social posts for all six platforms, and free SEO title options. The plans differ in how many generations you get, how long a piece can be, how many Brand Profiles you can save, and how much of the Brand Profile you can fill in.

## Plan comparison

| Plan | Price | Generations / mo | Words / generation | Brand Profiles |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | $0 | 6 | 1,500 | 1 |
| Basic | $29/mo, or $288/yr ($24/mo) | 50 | 3,000 | Up to 3 |
| Premium | $69/mo, or $708/yr ($59/mo) | 150 | 5,000 | Up to 10 |

<Note>
  All paid plans start with a 7-day free trial. No charge until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime.
</Note>

## What each plan unlocks

**Free**

- 6 generations per month, 1,500 words per piece, 1 Brand Profile
- Social posts for all six platforms, and five SEO title options per article
- Brand Profile basics: profile name, brand name, origin story, and highlight message

**Basic**

- Everything in Free, at 50 generations per month and 3,000 words per piece
- Up to 3 Brand Profiles
- The full Brand Profile: brand voice, personality, niche, what you do, content goal, target audience, website, and default CTA

**Premium**

- Everything in Basic, at 150 generations per month and 5,000 words per piece
- Up to 10 Brand Profiles
- SPEAK Boost extras, including TL;DR and FAQ sections, structure preferences, and a custom instruction, set per profile or per generation
- Writing DOs and DON'Ts that the writer follows on every piece

## Which one fits

- **Free.** Perfect for trying BrandVoice Pro on real content before you spend anything. No credit card required.
- **Basic.** The right fit for most solo business owners publishing consistently, with room for up to three brands.
- **Premium.** Built for higher volume, several brands, and full control over how each piece is shaped.

## Grandfathered free accounts

If you were a free user before the free-plan generation limit changed, your account keeps its earlier allowance of 10 generations per month. New free signups get 6 per month. The word limit is 1,500 either way.

## About generations

The generation pool is shared across articles and social posts. One article personalization uses one generation. One social post request uses one generation, regardless of how many platforms it covers. Full details are on [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained).

## Beta pricing

BrandVoice Pro is currently in beta. Subscribers who joined during beta hold a locked-in rate. See [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering) before you change plans, because any tier change forfeits that rate permanently.
