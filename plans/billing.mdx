BrandVoice Pro handles payments through Stripe. Your card details are entered and stored on Stripe's systems, never in the app.

## Starting a subscription

Start on the free plan with no credit card. When you want more generations, more Brand Profiles, or the full Brand Profile, open the pricing page in the app and select **Start Free Trial** on Basic or Premium.

- Every paid plan includes a **7-day free trial**, and you are not charged until the trial ends.
- Choose **monthly** or **yearly** billing before you check out. Yearly is billed once a year at the lower effective monthly rate.
- Promotion codes can be applied at checkout.

See [Plans & Pricing](/plans/plans-and-pricing) for current rates.

## Managing your subscription

Go to your profile page in the app and select **Manage subscription**. That opens the Stripe billing portal, where you can update your payment method, download invoices, switch plans, or cancel. Your plan and renewal date are shown on the profile page, along with a note if the subscription is set to end at the period close.

## Upgrading, downgrading, and cancelling

You can change plans at any time. Note how tier changes interact with grandfathered pricing:

<Warning>
  Any completed tier change, upgrade or downgrade, permanently forfeits grandfathered benefits. The new plan is billed and metered at the current published rate for that tier, and the earlier rate cannot be restored. Cancelling a paid plan returns your account to the current standard free plan. See [Beta & Grandfathered Pricing](/plans/beta-and-grandfathering).
</Warning>

When you cancel, your paid features stay active until the end of the period you already paid for. After that, your account moves to the free plan at 6 generations per month and 1 Brand Profile. Your saved profiles and history are not deleted, but you cannot create new profiles until you are back under the free limit.

## Questions about a charge

If something about a charge does not look right, email support with your account email and the date of the charge so the team can look into it quickly.
