BrandVoice Pro generates social posts for all six major platforms in one go: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Threads, and X. Each post is tailored to that platform's tone and format, not the same caption pasted six times.

## How to generate social posts

You can create social posts two ways:

- **Alongside an article.** When you personalize a piece, generate the matching social posts as part of the same workflow.
- **On their own.** Request social posts independently when you just need to fill your social calendar.

Either way, the posts use your Brand Profile, so they carry your voice and speak to your audience the same way your articles do.

## One request, one generation

A single social post request counts as one generation, no matter how many platforms it covers. Generating posts for all six platforms still uses one generation from your monthly pool. See [Generations Explained](/plans/generations-explained) for how the shared pool works.

<Tip>
  Because posts are tailored per platform, you can publish them as-is or use them as strong first drafts. A LinkedIn post reads like LinkedIn; a TikTok caption reads like TikTok.
</Tip>
